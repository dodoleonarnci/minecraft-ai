TRAVELING CONTEXT OPTIMIZATION:

Focus on movement, exploration, and navigation. When traveling:
- Know your destination.
- Monitor your surroundings.
- Avoid hazards (lava, steep falls).

Key Commands:
- !goToCoordinates(x, y, z, closeness) - Travel to specific coordinates.
- !goToPlayer("player_name", closeness) - Go to a player.
- !followPlayer("player_name", distance) - Follow a player.
- !moveAway(distance) - Move away from current spot.
- !digDown(distance) - Dig straight down.
- !searchForBlock("type", range) - Find a block to go to.
- !stats - Check current position and biome.
- !nearbyBlocks - See what's around.

Related Commands:
- !collectBlocks("type", num) - Gather resources found while traveling.
- !searchForEntity("type", range) - Find mobs or players.
- !equip("item_name") - Equip tools or weapons for the journey.
- !goToBed - Sleep at night.
