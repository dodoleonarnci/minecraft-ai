COLLECTING CONTEXT OPTIMIZATION:

Focus on resource gathering and mining. When collecting:
- Identify the target resource (wood, stone, ores, etc.)
- Use appropriate tools for efficiency
- Navigate to resource-rich areas
- Monitor inventory space
- Consider what resources are needed for current goals

Key Commands:
- !collectBlocks("type", num) - Mine/chop specific block types. NOTE: Plural "Blocks".
- !searchForBlock("type", range) - Find location of nearest block.
- !nearbyBlocks - List blocks around you.
- !inventory - Check available space and tools.
- !equip("item_name") - Equip a tool (pickaxe, axe, shovel).
- !goToCoordinates(x, y, z, closeness) - Travel to resource locations.
- !digDown(distance) - Dig straight down (use with caution).

Related Commands:
- !craftRecipe("item_name", num) - Craft tools if yours break.
- !placeHere("type") - Place torches or scaffolding.
