HUNTING CONTEXT OPTIMIZATION:

Focus on combat, defense, and gathering food from mobs. When hunting:
- Equip your best weapon and armor.
- Locate targets.
- Manage health and hunger.

Key Commands:
- !attack("entity_type", kill_boolean) - Attack a mob (e.g., "zombie", "sheep").
- !attackPlayer("player_name", kill_boolean) - Attack a player (use with caution).
- !searchForEntity("type", range) - Find mobs or players.
- !entities - List nearby entities.
- !equip("item_name") - Equip sword, axe, or armor.
- !consume("item_name") - Eat food to heal.
- !stats - Check health and hunger.

Related Commands:
- !collectBlocks("type", num) - Gather blocks to build defenses.
- !craftRecipe("item_name", num) - Craft weapons or armor.
- !goToBed - Sleep to skip the night/set spawn.
