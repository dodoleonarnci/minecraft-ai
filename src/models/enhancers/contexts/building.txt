BUILDING CONTEXT OPTIMIZATION:

Focus on construction and placement tasks. When building:
- Plan the structure layout.
- Ensure you have enough materials.
- Clear the area if needed.

Key Commands:
- !placeHere("type") - Place a block at your current location.
- !collectBlocks("type", num) - Gather building materials or clear space.
- !inventory - Check material counts.
- !equip("item_name") - Equip blocks or tools.
- !goToCoordinates(x, y, z, closeness) - Move to build positions.
- !nearbyBlocks - Check what's around you.

Related Commands:
- !craftRecipe("item_name", num) - Craft building blocks (planks, bricks) or tools.
- !searchForBlock("type", range) - Find specific blocks to build near.
